About

Story of YouDownloader

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started off as a team of dedicated students with the urge to watch coding tutorials offline after we left the school. Tools to use were either paid for, or too slow and with many ads and unnecessary content. We had to work around and make one for ourselves sice no one could do it. 

  • March 2011

    YouDownloader is Born

    We started making great progress and due to the feedback we were receiving. We decided to tart our organisation to cater for our own and the community's software needs.

  • December 2018

    Transition to Full Service

    We made software for different companies, organisations, institutions and individuals and we qualified to be a full software designing organisation with great reputation and top-notch designs.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    Nothing is big to beat. And so we want to expand and make some more free to use software. We target markets and areas where most companies take advantage and exploit the general public due to their lack of technology knowhow
